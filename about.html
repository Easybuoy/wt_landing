Culture At Trackdrills

Headquartered in the heart of Greece, Trackdrills is a successful, self-funded, start-up that operates on a fully remote basis, and with an essential emphasis on personal development. Spread throughout Europe, and Africa, our team casts a wide net across a multitude of locations, lifestyles and backgrounds. It’s our differences that make us interesting, and our shared belief in Trackdrills core values that bind us together.


Trackdrills's culture is guided by respect, transparency, collaboration and direct feedback. We have no room on our team for disrespect, office politics or discrimination of any kind. We're obsessed with communicating with our users as well as within the team.

We’re a distributed company

Just like our products, our company finds strength in distribution. For us, it’s critical to building a great company and great products.

Open source, it’s our heart

Our company is built around open source, which builds a community that keeps everyone honest in how we engineer products that users know and love.

Our teams have character(s)

From engineering to HR operations, product marketing and legal, learn what makes our teams tick, which is the right fit for you, and where opportunities await.
