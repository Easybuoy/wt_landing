Culture At Trackdrills

Headquartered in the heart of Greece, Trackdrills is a successful, self-funded, start-up that operates on a fully remote basis, and with an essential emphasis on personal development. Spread throughout Europe, and Africa, our team casts a wide net across a multitude of locations, lifestyles and backgrounds. It’s our differences that make us interesting, and our shared belief in Hotjar’s core values that bind us together. Trackdrills's culture is guided by respect, transparency, collaboration and direct feedback. We have no room on our team for disrespect, office politics or discrimination of any kind. We're obsessed with communicating with our users as well as within the team.
